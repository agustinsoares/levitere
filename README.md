# levitere

**Hero**

Cinematic visual experiences for a new era of property marketing
Levitere creates cinematic visual content for properties, architecture and selected spaces, combining FPV drone filmmaking, video, photography and digital 3D.

Our goal is to make properties easier to understand, more immersive to experience and more memorable to future clients.

**About us**

Levitere is a young creative studio founded by Oscar and Emilio, aged 22 and 23, after meeting in Bergen, Norway.

Oscar is an FPV drone pilot. Emilio is a photographer, videographer and editor. What started as a friendship became a creative partnership built around a shared way of seeing spaces, movement and visual storytelling.

Together, we created Levitere to produce high end audiovisual content for real estate, architecture, brands and selected spaces that need to be presented with a more modern and immersive visual language.

Our approach combines a young creative perspective with technical precision, bringing together drone flight, video, photography, editing and digital 3D craft to help each project communicate with clarity, atmosphere and impact.
